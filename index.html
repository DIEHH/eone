WHO WE ARE

WHY WE’RE HERE

Eone was founded with an innovative spirit and an unwavering mission: to create fashionable products that are accessible for everyone. Our name Eone is short for everyone, reflecting our desire to serve everyone. Our debut product – The Bradley Timepiece – was created to accommodate both sighted and vision impaired users equally. Since The Bradley doesn’t require sight to tell time, we call it a timepiece instead of a watch. Not only are our timepieces fashionable for everyone, they are functional for everyone, too.

OUR INSPIRATION

The Bradley is named after Brad Snyder. An ex naval-officer, Brad lost his eyesight in an explosion in Afghanistan in 2011. Brad competed in and won gold and silver medals in swimming at the 2012 Paralympics in London.
